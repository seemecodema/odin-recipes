Corn Pudding

Description

Corn pudding is an old Kershner family recipe usually enjoyed on Thanksgiving or Christmas. It is similar to cornbread but has the consistency of bread pudding.

All dairy ingredients may be substituted for non-dairy alternatives with little difference in the final result. Some older versions of the recipe call for cheddar cheese to be added to the top during the last 15 minutes of baking. This has been omitted here, as it is disgusting.

Ingredients

  • 1 can whole corn
  • 1 can creamed corn
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 package corn muffin mix

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients and pour into ungreasaed pan
  2. Bake at 350Â° for 90 minutes - the middle will be wet but will set when cool
  3. Allow to cool for at least 15 minutes, serve warm or cold
