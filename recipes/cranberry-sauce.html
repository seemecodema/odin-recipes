Cranberry Sauce

Description

Many people prefer canned cranberry sauce during the holidays - these people are wrong. Homemade cranberry sauce is extremely easy to make and vastly superior to a can-shaped brick of gelatin.

This recipe utilizes orange zest - while some may be tempted to skip this step it is strongly recommended not to do so as it greatly enhances the flavor. Simple yet effective touches like this separate civilized folk from beasts who need only a can opener to facilitate “good eating.”

Ingredients

  • 1 bag cranberries (12oz)
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon grated orange zest
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Wash cranberries and discard any that are mushy or unripe
  2. Combine all ingredients in a saucepan an bring to a boil
  3. Reduce to a simmer and cook until cranberries are soft, about 10 minutes
